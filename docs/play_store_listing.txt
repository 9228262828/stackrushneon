Enter the neon rush and test your timing in Stack Rush Neon, a fast offline arcade stacking game.

Tap at the perfect moment to place each moving block on the tower. Accurate drops keep your tower wide, while imperfect drops cut away the overhanging section and make every next move more challenging.

Build combos with perfect hits, increase your score, and try to beat your personal best.
