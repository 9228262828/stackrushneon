Privacy Policy — Stack Rush Neon

Last updated: JYLY, 2026

Stack Rush Neon is an offline arcade game designed to provide a fast, enjoyable, and secure gaming experience. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and the choices available to you when using the application.

1. Information We Collect

Stack Rush Neon does not require registration, login, or the creation of a user account.

The app does not collect, transmit, sell, rent, or share personal information.

The app does not request access to your name, email address, contacts, precise location, photos, camera, microphone, payment information, advertising identifiers, or social-media accounts.

2. Local Data Storage

The app stores a limited amount of information locally on your device using SharedPreferences. This may include your best score and your sound, music, and haptic preferences.

This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to an external server. Deleting the app or clearing its data may permanently remove this information.

3. Internet Connection

Stack Rush Neon is designed to work fully offline. The app does not require an internet connection for gameplay and does not connect to a backend server.

4. Advertising and Analytics

The app does not contain third-party advertising, advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, tracking technologies, behavioral profiling, or cross-app tracking.

5. Third-Party Services

Stack Rush Neon does not use Firebase, remote databases, cloud storage, social login, advertising platforms, or third-party analytics services.

6. Children's Privacy

Stack Rush Neon is suitable for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the applicable age defined by local laws. The application contains no chat features, social interaction, online multiplayer services, or user-generated content.

7. Data Security

Stack Rush Neon does not collect or transmit personal information to external servers. All game preferences and progress stored by the application remain locally on your device. While no software can guarantee absolute security, we take reasonable measures to ensure that the application stores local data safely using standard platform storage mechanisms.

8. Your Choices

You can change sound, music, and haptic preferences inside the Settings screen. You can reset the best score there as well. You can remove all local data by clearing app data or uninstalling the app.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the app features or legal requirements change. The updated policy will remain available inside the app or with the app's published documentation.
