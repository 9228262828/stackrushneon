# Privacy Policy — Stack Rush Neon

**Last updated: JYLY, 2026**

Stack Rush Neon is an offline arcade game developed to provide a simple and entertaining stacking experience.

## 1. Information We Collect

Stack Rush Neon does not require registration, login, or the creation of a user account.

The app does not collect, transmit, sell, rent, or share personal information.

The app does not request access to:

- Your name or email address
- Contacts
- Precise location
- Photos or media
- Camera
- Microphone
- Payment information
- Device identifiers for advertising
- Social media accounts

## 2. Local Data Storage

The app stores a limited amount of information locally on your device using SharedPreferences.

This local information may include:

- Best score
- Sound preference
- Music preference
- Haptic feedback preference

This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to any external server.

Deleting the app or clearing the app data may permanently remove this locally stored information.

## 3. Internet Connection

Stack Rush Neon is designed to work fully offline.

The app does not require an internet connection for gameplay and does not connect to a backend server.

## 4. Advertising and Analytics

The app does not contain:

- Third-party advertising
- Advertising SDKs
- Analytics SDKs
- Tracking technologies
- Behavioral profiling
- Cross-app tracking

## 5. Third-Party Services

Stack Rush Neon does not use Firebase, remote databases, cloud storage, social login, advertising platforms, or third-party analytics services.

The app may rely on standard operating-system features for local storage and haptic feedback.

## 6. Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children or adults.

The app does not include:

- Chat
- User-generated content
- Public profiles
- Online multiplayer
- Social interaction
- In-app purchases

Parents and guardians should supervise device usage according to their own family rules.

## 7. Data Security

Because the app does not collect or transmit personal information, no personal data is stored on external servers.

Local preferences are protected by the normal security mechanisms of your device and operating system.

## 8. Your Choices

You can change sound, music, and haptic preferences inside the Settings screen.

You can reset the best score from the Settings screen.

You can remove all local app data by clearing the app data through your device settings or uninstalling the app.

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the app features or legal requirements change.

The updated policy will remain available inside the app or with the app's published documentation.

## 10. Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer using the support email published on the app's Google Play Store listing.
